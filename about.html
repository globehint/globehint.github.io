Who's behind this

Everything you need, in one guide.

Globehint is written, researched, and built by one traveler, not a team. Here's the story, and what to expect from it.

Globehint started because most travel guides read like they were written by a committee, or worse, by no one at all. I wanted something that read like one person actually went somewhere, paid attention, and wrote it down properly.

So that's what this is. Every guide is researched and written by me, with no contributors and no outsourced content, even on the ones I haven't visited myself yet. Some come from a trip I've actually taken. Others come from a network of people I trust who have, combined with proper digging through blogs, forums, and review sites. Either way, nothing goes up without real work behind it.

What kept bothering me about other guides was how incomplete they always seemed. One would cover restaurants but say nothing about getting around. Another would nail the sights but skip what to actually pack or sort out beforehand. I wanted everything you need for a trip, and for preparing for it, sitting in one place instead of spread across a dozen open tabs.

What Globehint isn't

  • Like other travel guides. Everything you need for a trip is included and deep research ensures this.
  • Not a syndicate. There's no editorial team, just one person and whatever time I have between trips.
  • Not always firsthand. Some guides come from a trip I've taken myself, others from trusted contacts and deep research. The research page below explains how each one works.
  • Not infallible. Places close, prices change, and I get things wrong sometimes. That's what the bug report below is for.
